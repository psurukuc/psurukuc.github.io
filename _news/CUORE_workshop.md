The Pitt's CUORE group hosted the CUORE Analaysis Workshop on November 19-21, 2025. The meeting held in William Pitt Union brought together CUORE collaborators to discuss the plans for the upcoming analysis of CUORE in search for neutrinoless double beta decay and other physics topics with 3 ton.yr of exposure.
